# Story Sequence Strategy (Nicolas Clay / Clay Consulting)

Decoded from IG reel **DSH20zdjNqN** (≈120s) + his lead-magnet Notion doc. Creator: **Nicolas Clay**, Clay Consulting (coaches scaling coaches to $100k/mo). His teaching reel format = split-screen talking head + animated dark board; 0 hard cuts.

## The idea
A **story sequence** = a set of **exactly 5 Instagram story slides** that move a viewer hook → action. His pitch: most people reuse ONE template (Pain→Desire→Proof) every time, which only fires a couple of "buying triggers." Fix = a library of **7 frameworks**, each tuned to a different psychological lever; run a different one each day so by Sunday you've hit every trigger. (All 7 are a uniform 5-beat spine — verified off his board. "Storytelling" can repeat across 2 slides, e.g. Connection.)

## The 7 frameworks (each = 5 slides, in order)
1. **Pain** — Pain Hook · Agitate · Proof · Desire · Break Objection
2. **Your Proof** — Personal Win Hook · Pain · Agitate · Proof · Relatability Hook
3. **Client Proof** — Client Win Hook · Relatability · Pain · Agitate · Proof
4. **Break Objection** — Curiosity Hook · Break Obj · Storytelling · Shift Mind · Action-Driven CTA
5. **Education** — Pain Hook · Educate · Proof · Desire · Desire CTA
6. **Differentiation** ("Differenciation" on his board) — Storytelling Hook · Different · Proof · Desire · Break Objection
7. **Connection** — Authentic Hook · Storytelling · Storytelling · Proof · CTA

Common spine across all: hook → tension → proof → push; Proof sits mid-most; most end on a CTA or Break-Objection.

## His weekly rotation + buying triggers (HIS strategic layer — we stripped this for the reusable board)
Mon **Pain**=urgency · Tue **Your Proof**=authority · Wed **Client Proof**=social proof · Thu **Break Objection**=objection removal · Fri **Education**=trust by value · Sat **Differentiation**=stand apart · Sun **Connection**=bond. Animation = each day lights one more brain region; whole brain lit by Sunday.

## His 3-step build workflow
Choose framework → Write copy (Google Doc) → Create story (lay into 5 slides, post).

## The funnel (the frameworks are the lead magnet)
Reel → comment **"framework"** → auto-DM with a Notion link → "Story Sequence Strategy" doc (one-screen teaser, re-lists 7 types) → **Calendly**: "free 45-min consulting session, worth $2.7k, this month only, if you qualify" → qualification call → join Clay Consulting.
